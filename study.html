Visual Analysis of Big Data

It's an undergraduate research opportunity program(UROP), which aims to provide some research experience for undergraduate students

The goal of this project is to develop a website that can provide information about passengers flow in the campus especially restaurants and bus stations. The observation of population is based on WiFi connection in this area. Intuitively, larger number of WiFi connection related to crowded area. The is observation is updated every 5 minutes./n Besides observation, I also developed a rough prediction of future population by some naive machine learning methods.
